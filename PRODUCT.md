# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Threadline is used by parents and guardians who are preparing structured ADHD assessment material for a child. They may also coordinate with teachers, carers, and clinicians while collecting questionnaires, documents, and assessment package context.

## Product Purpose

Threadline helps families create a clear child profile, collect assessment inputs, prepare an Assessment Package, and share supporting information with permission. Success means a parent understands what is needed next and can move through the assessment workflow with trust, calm, and clarity.

## Brand Personality

Warm, precise, and reassuring. The interface should feel clinically credible without becoming cold, and family-friendly without becoming childish.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative healthcare cliches, vague wellness dashboards, aggressive sales checkout patterns, and legal language that feels hidden or coercive. Do not make clinical actions feel like casual social sharing.

## Design Principles

- Ask for permission at the moment it matters.
- Make clinical complexity feel ordered and human.
- Prefer plain-language confidence over marketing flourish.
- Keep parent actions visible, reversible, and easy to understand.
- Preserve existing app patterns before inventing new UI.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use clear labels, high-contrast text, keyboard-accessible controls, visible focus states, and plain-language copy. Motion should be brief and should respect reduced-motion preferences through existing component patterns.
